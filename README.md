# Marketing Performance Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Marketing Performance Analytics Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze marketing campaign effectiveness, channel performance, customer engagement, and return on investment (ROI).

The dashboard enables marketing teams and business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, compare channel effectiveness, and make data-driven decisions for budget optimization and campaign planning.

---

## Business Problem

Marketing teams invest significant budgets across multiple channels such as Email, Social Media, Search Engines, and Direct Mail. Understanding which channels generate the highest returns and conversions is critical for maximizing marketing efficiency.

This dashboard helps answer:

* Which marketing channel generates the highest revenue?
* Which region performs best?
* How effective are campaigns in converting customers?
* What is the overall ROI on marketing investments?
* How does revenue vary across quarters and channels?

### Conversion Performance

| Channel       | Conversions |
